Privacy Policy

Rickshaw Wala — by Daynix Studio

Last updated: 22 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game Rickshaw Wala ("the App", "we", "our") handles information. By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

Short version: Rickshaw Wala works fully offline. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. No account or sign-up is required.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Specifically, the App does not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, or files from your device
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs

No registration, login, or account is required to play.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The App saves your game progress locally on your own device only. This includes:

  • High score, total coins, distance, and games played
  • Unlocked rickshaws and city maps
  • Sound and music settings

This data never leaves your device, is not transmitted to us or to any third party, and is deleted when you uninstall the App. You can also clear it at any time from the App's Settings screen using "Reset Progress".

3. Internet Access

The App is designed to work completely offline. It does not require an internet connection to play.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the Google Play In-App Review feature, which may occasionally show an official Google rating prompt. This feature is provided by Google and is governed by Google's own privacy policy, available at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

The App is distributed through Google Play. Google may collect certain information as part of app distribution, independently of us.

5. Advertising

The App does not display advertisements and does not use advertising or analytics SDKs.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences, including children. Because we do not collect any personal information from anyone, we do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Data Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is no personal information at risk of being exposed through the App. Locally saved game progress is protected by your device's own security.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
